    Phoenix Conservatory of Music
  • -The Phoenix Conservatory of Music (PCM) is non-profit community music school that offers classes to learn, create and perform music. PCM has recently started music classes at Paradise community college, and is partnered with Grand Canyon University and Berklee College of Music and The Berklee City Music Network in Boston Massachusetts.

  • -In middle school I learned how to read and play music starting with the clarinet. continued my music education at PCM beginning with guitar and bass. I was actively involved with PCM for over 4 years. I was accepted into the Berklee College of Music college prep program where I learned how to practice and perform in music ensembles. I worked in groups of students to generate music performances resulting in a variety of music genres. We worked together several times a week to understand the theory of music, which is the writing, reading and comprehension of music. We also learned audio production, which included producing music using computer software to cleanup music and create new music. I learned about the timing of music and how to listen by ear to create music, We performed in several venues including the Nash, Salvation Center KROC Center, Desert Rose Steakhouse and many others. I graduated from the program in 2018.
